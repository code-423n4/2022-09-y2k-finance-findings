# Custom Errors

## Details
Starting from Solidity v0.8.4, using custom errors is a gas-efficient wat to explain to users why an operation failed. See reference: https://blog.soliditylang.org/2021/04/21/custom-errors/

## Mitigation
I suggest making a custom error for example
`error AddressZero();`

# Pre-increment cost less gas than post-increment

## Details
`i++` costs more gas than `++i` , for uint pre-decrement is cheaper than post-decrement
see reference: https://github.com/code-423n4/2021-12-nftx-findings/issues/195

## Mitigation
change `i++` to `++i`
